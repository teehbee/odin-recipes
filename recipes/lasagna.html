Tor's Orgasmic Lasagna

This delicious lasagna will simply f*ck you in your mouth

This is the one recipe that I have perfected over the years, making it better and better.

Adjusting and tweaking it until I simply could not make it any better.

This juicy and meatfilled lasagna will simply penetrate your tastebuds and leave you wanting for more.

Shit you need

  • 500 grams of minced meat. Preferrably a mix of pork and beef
  • 1 400 gram can of good tomatoes
  • 1 large yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large carrot, diced
  • 3 stalks of celery, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato puree
  • 1 dl of water
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 6 dl of whole milk
  • Lots of cheese
  • Lasagna sheets, either homemade or store bought if you are a cheater
  • Lots of herbs and seasoning and shit

Shit you have to do

  1. First you make a delicious meat sauce by browning your mince in a pan on high heat. As it browns,
    add the onion, garlic, celery and carrot and cook until softened.
  2. Add the pureed tomato and fry the shit out of it for about 30 seconds to get the bitterness out of it.
  3. Add the canned tomatoes, water, some delicious herbs, and let the sauce cook for as long as you have
    patience for.
  4. In a seperate pot, make your bechamel sauce by melting the butter before you add and mix in the flour.
    Mix in the milk a little at the time and make a lovely thick white sauce. At the end, mix in
    a fistful of cheese together with salt, pepper and nutmeg.
  5. You are now ready to layer this shit. Star with a layer of meat sauce in a buttered ovenproof pan.
    Pour some of your cheese sauce on top of the meat sauce before you add a layer of pasta sheets.
    Continue this way layer by until you are all out of shit.
  6. Sprinke the reminder of your cheese on top, before you put the fucker in the oven and cook it for about 25-30 minutes
    at 220 degrees celcius or until perfectly gratinated.
